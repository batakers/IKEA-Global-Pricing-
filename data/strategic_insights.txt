IKEA Global Pricing Strategy - Strategic Insights

5 Strategic Business Insights:
1. Egypt is the most expensive market with average price $264.75, while Malaysia is the cheapest at $118.02.
2. Price positioning is most premium in Egypt (price index 1.53) and most value-oriented in Malaysia (price index 0.68).
3. Affordability pressure is highest in Egypt (index 0.0683) and lowest in Qatar (index 0.0017).
4. Egypt shows the highest price dispersion (std 466.83), indicating mixed pricing tiers or assortment mix.
5. United States has the widest assortment breadth (177 sub-categories), while digital availability is weakest in Egypt (66.9% online).

3 Strategic Recommendations:
1. Introduce market-specific entry-price SKUs in Egypt and similar high-pressure markets to improve affordability without eroding global brand value.
2. Review premium pricing justification in Egypt using competitor benchmarks and elasticity testing to protect volume growth.
3. Prioritize e-commerce enablement in Egypt and low-online peers to expand reach, especially for categories with strong global demand.
